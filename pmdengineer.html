Meet James Cuevas

Owner and Principal Engineer at Precision Mechanical Design, LLC

James Cuevas obtained a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of California, Santa Barbara in 1997, and has been designing mechanical devices, products, and systems on a daily basis ever since.

Since going into full-time consulting as PMD in 2005, James has worked on numerous projects across various industries, dealing with all types of challenging design problems. James has maintained a razor-sharp focus on mechanical design engineering throughout his career, and has found that working in various industries simultaneously has proven to be an asset.
